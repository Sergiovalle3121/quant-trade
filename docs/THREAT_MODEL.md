# Threat Model

Scope is research, backtesting, simulated paper trading, paper broker integration, cloud dry-run workflows, evidence, and approvals.

## Threats

- Secrets committed to configs, logs, reports, or artifacts.
- Accidental use of live broker endpoints or real-money approvals.
- Missing or tampered audit evidence.
- Misleading claims about legal, financial, or investment readiness.

## Controls

Local secret scanning, redaction, permission checks, audit review, compliance-style reports, and paper-only documentation.
