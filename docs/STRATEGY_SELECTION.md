# Strategy Selection

Candidate selection reads local research artifacts and applies conservative promotion criteria. Missing required metrics fail closed. Selection considers benchmark excess return, drawdown, turnover, train/test Sharpe gap, out-of-sample months, robustness flags, and red flags rather than best Sharpe alone.

Promotion requires human approval notes, research artifacts, risk limits, an enabled kill switch, and confirmation that no live broker integration is configured. Passing promotion means simulated paper readiness only, not profitability.
